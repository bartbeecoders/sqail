# sqail Press Kit

Journalists, bloggers, and podcasters: everything you need to write about sqail is in this folder. Copy anything, no permission required. If you want a quote or a walkthrough, open an issue on [Codeberg](https://codeberg.org/bartbeecoders/sqail) or [GitHub](https://github.com/bartbeecoders/sqail).

## Contents

- `one-liner.md` — the one-sentence pitch
- `descriptions.md` — short, medium, and long descriptions ready to paste
- `boilerplate.md` — "About sqail" block for the bottom of an article
- `facts.md` — numbers, dates, and verifiable claims
- `quotes.md` — quotable lines from the maintainer
- `assets/` — logos, screenshots, and social cards
  - `sqail-snail-transparent.png` — 512×512 blue snail mark on a transparent background (drop on light or dark; do not recolor)

## Name & pronunciation

**sqail** — always lowercase in prose. Pronounced *"snail."* The play on SQL + snail is deliberate: small, deliberate, carries its house with it.

Never write "Sqail", "SQaiL", "sequel", or "S-Q-A-I-L." If you want to style the name in a logo, the wordmark uses title case inside the asset — that styling does not carry into body copy.

## Usage rules

- Use the sqail name and logo freely in editorial coverage.
- Do not imply endorsement, partnership, or affiliation without asking first.
- Do not modify the logo (no recoloring, stretching, rotation, or outlines).
- Screenshots in this kit are released under the same MIT license as the product.
