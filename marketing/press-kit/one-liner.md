# One-liner

> A fast, small, open-source SQL editor that makes AI-assisted querying feel native.

## Variants (in order of formality)

- **Casual:** "A 20 MB SQL client with AI built in — not bolted on."
- **Technical:** "A Tauri-based cross-platform SQL editor with schema-aware NL-to-SQL, no telemetry, and a sub-second cold start."
- **Elevator:** "sqail is DBeaver without the JVM, DataGrip without the price tag, and TablePlus without the lock-in — open source and under 20 megabytes."
