# Quotes

Pre-approved quotes from the maintainer. Use in articles, roundups, or podcast show notes. No need to ask — just attribute to "Bart, maintainer of sqail."

## On the project's goal

> "Every developer and analyst deserves a free, fast, privacy-respecting SQL editor that treats AI as a built-in tool, not a paid add-on. sqail is that tool."

## On why another SQL client

> "DBeaver is free but bloated. DataGrip is polished but expensive. TablePlus is fast but paid and macOS-first. sqail is the one that's all three: small, cross-platform, and free — with AI that actually knows your schema."

## On AI integration

> "The hard part of NL-to-SQL isn't the language model. It's the schema. sqail injects your real tables and columns into the prompt, so 'top customers last quarter' resolves against your database, not a guess."

## On privacy

> "Privacy isn't a marketing line for us. sqail ships zero telemetry, stores credentials locally, and only talks to AI providers you configured yourself. If that's important to you, you don't have to take our word for it — the source is public."

## On open source

> "I want sqail to outlive me. That's why it's MIT, why it's on Codeberg, and why there will never be a paid tier that holds features hostage. Grow with us or fork us — both are fine."
